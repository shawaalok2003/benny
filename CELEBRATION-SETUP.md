## Required Files

To complete the birthday celebration experience, please add the following files:

### 1. Birthday Celebration Image
- **Location**: `public/birthday-celebration.png`
- **Description**: A fun, colorful birthday image (cake, balloons, party scene, etc.)
- **Recommended Size**: 600x600 pixels or similar
- **Format**: PNG (for transparency) or JPG
- **Note**: If the image is not found, colorful birthday emojis (ðŸŽ‚ðŸŽ‰ðŸŽˆ) will be displayed as a fallback

### 2. Birthday Music Audio
- **Location**: `public/sounds/happy-birthday.mp3`
- **Description**: Happy Birthday song or celebratory music
- **Format**: MP3
- **Note**: The music will play automatically when the celebration appears at score 11 and will loop until the user continues

### 3. Optional: Ball Bounce Sound
- **Location**: `public/sounds/bounce.mp3`
- **Description**: Sound effect for when ball bounces on the paddle
- **Format**: MP3

## Quick Setup

1. Create the `public/sounds/` directory if it doesn't exist
2. Add your audio files to `public/sounds/`
3. Add your celebration image directly to the `public/` folder
4. Restart the development server if needed
